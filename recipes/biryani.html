Biryani

Description

This aromatic biryani is rich, flavorful, and full of traditional spices. Tender chicken pieces layered with fragrant basmati rice, caramelized onions, and saffron create the perfect blend of taste and aroma.

Perfect for family dinners, festivals, or special occasions, this biryani brings authentic South Asian flavors to your table. Serve it with raita, salad, and lemon slices for a complete meal!

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger & garlic paste
  • Green chilies
  • Fresh coriander & mint
  • Oil or ghee
  • Biryani masala / whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaf)
  • Saffron or food color
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 20_30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee and fry sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken, ginger-garlic paste, and spices; cook until chicken changes color.
  4. Add tomatoes, yogurt, green chilies, and herbs; cook until oil separates.
  5. Boil rice until 70% cooked, then drain the water.
  6. Layer rice and chicken mixture in a pot; sprinkle saffron or food color.
  7. Cover and steam (dum) on low heat for 20_30 minutes.
  8. Gently mix before serving.
