Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is rich, hearty, and full of flavor. Layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese come together to create a comforting and delicious meal.

Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this lasagna combines traditional Italian flavors with a warm, homemade touch. Serve it with garlic bread and a fresh salad for the perfect meal!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion & garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender.
  2. Cook ground beef with onion and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasoning, then simmer.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  6. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30_40 minutes.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
