Pasta

Description

This creamy pasta is rich, comforting, and full of flavor! Made with tender pasta tossed in a smooth and cheesy sauce, it's the perfect dish for quick lunches or cozy dinners.

Whether you're cooking for family or treating yourself, this pasta brings restaurant-style taste right to your home. Serve it with garlic bread and a sprinkle of parmesan for the best experience!

Ingredients

  • Pasta (penne, spaghetti, or your choice)
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Garlic (finely chopped)
  • Onion (optional, finely chopped)
  • Chicken cubes or veggies (optional)
  • Milk or cream
  • Cheese (parmesan or mozzarella)
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Chili flakes
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft, then drain.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan and saut√© garlic (and onion if added).
  3. Add chicken or vegetables and cook until done (optional).
  4. Pour milk/cream and mix well to create sauce.
  5. Add cheese, salt, pepper, chili flakes, and oregano; stir until creamy.
  6. Add boiled pasta and toss until fully coated in sauce.
  7. Simmer for 2_3 minutes, then turn off heat.
  8. Serve hot with extra cheese on top!
