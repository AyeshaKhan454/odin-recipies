Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is cheesy, delicious, and packed with flavor! With a soft crust, rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and your favorite toppings, it brings classic pizzeria taste right to your kitchen.

Perfect for weekend dinners, movie nights, or parties, this pizza is easy to customize. Serve hot with garlic dip, chili flakes, and oregano for the ultimate pizza experience!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce (or homemade tomato sauce)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Chili flakes
  • Salt & pepper
  • Vegetables (capsicum, onion, mushrooms)
  • Chicken or pepperoni (optional)
  • Sweet corn (optional)
  • Black olives (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and brush lightly with olive oil.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add vegetables, chicken/pepperoni, or your desired toppings.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously on top.
  6. Bake for 12_15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese melts.
  7. Remove from oven, sprinkle oregano & chili flakes, and slice.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
